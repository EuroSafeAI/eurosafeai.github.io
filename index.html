Association Details

Legal Form & Purpose

Under the name "EuroSafeAI" there exists an association pursuant to Article 60 et seqq. of the Swiss Civil Code (CC).

The association is politically independent and denominationally neutral. It does not pursue any profit-making or self-help purposes.
