Legal Basis

Under the name "EuroSafeAI" there exists an association pursuant to Article 60 et seqq. CC.

Headquarters

Brähenstrasse 4, Uetikon am See.

Nature

It is politically independent and denominationally neutral. The association is exclusively non-profit and does not pursue any profit-making or self-help purposes. Its bodies act in a voluntary capacity.

Objective and Purpose

The association's purpose is to deepen humanity's understanding of intelligent systems and to promote the highest standards in their development and deployment.
